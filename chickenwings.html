Chicken Wing Recipie

There are three types of wings you'll find at the market: the whole wing, the flat, and the drumette (the latter being the part that looks like a mini-drumstick). When you see these two parts together, it's easy to imagine the wing of a bird.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine the olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine. Add the chicken wings; reseal and shake to coat. Arrange the chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  3. Cook the wings in the preheated oven 1 hour, or until crisp and cooked through.

Nutrition Facts!

Per Serving: 532 calories; protein 31.7g; carbohydrates 3.9g; fat 43.1g; cholesterol 96.6mg; sodium 122.6mg.
